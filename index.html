About Us

Bharat Netaji Foundation develops volunteers for social change, promoting leadership, engagement and social progress. Through structured development, capacity building and grassroots activities, volunteers lead sustainable social initiatives. Our mission: To connect you with local causes like education, health, sustainable development, youth leadership and inspire change. Join us to create a better tomorrow!

Our Work

  • Organizing awareness programs to increase public participation in social initiatives.
  • Establishing BNF volunteer units at the State, District, and Village levels to expand service reach.
  • Conducting Netaji Leadership Training Camps for the next generation youth.
  • Fostering a society that promotes patriotism and national unity.
  • Empowering youth to become nation-building leaders of tomorrow.
  • Enhancing the lives of the poor through education and social welfare.
  • Creating a safe and encouraging environment for children and women.
  • Celebrating Netaji Memorial Day, national events, and awareness observances.
  • Building a New India – a strong and socially responsible young Bharat with self-reliance and unity.

Vision

  • National unity and patriotism
  • Social welfare and public support
  • Consistent social engagement and activities
  • Motivating youth to join the armed forces
  • Workshops on patriotism and community service
  • Bringing awareness about government welfare schemes to rural areas
  • Sports competitions in the name of Netaji
  • Educational support including books and scholarships
  • Village development projects – Solar lights, sanitation, and healthcare
  • Netaji Awards – Recognition for outstanding social service
  • Netaji Library / Historical Archives collection drive
  • Free medical consultation & 25% assistance for police & army personnel
  • Free Silambam classes across all districts
  • Free ambulance service for the poor and unwell
  • Free funeral van for the underprivileged
