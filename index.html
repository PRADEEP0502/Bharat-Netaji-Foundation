Let’s unite with one vision

Bharat Netaji Foundation was established with the core mission of carrying forward the patriotism, sacrifice, and leadership qualities of Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose to the younger generation. The foundation is actively involved in promoting progress in education, social welfare, and national unity. It aims to transform youth into self-aware and socially responsible citizens, encouraging them to contribute to the service of the nation through active participation and commitment.

Who we are

Bharat Netaji Foundation develops volunteers for social change, promoting leadership, engagement and social progress. Through structured development, capacity building and grassroots activities, volunteers lead sustainable social initiatives. Our mission: To connect you with local causes like education, health, sustainable development, youth leadership and inspire change. Join us to create a better tomorrow!

Our Vision

  • To instill the values of discipline, honesty, justice, and leadership in the younger generation through army-style training and programs.
  • To spread awareness about the patriotic contributions and sacrifices of kings and freedom fighters.
  • To conduct awareness programs in schools and colleges.
  • To provide training about patriotism and freedom fighters.
  • To conduct blood donation camps and other social service programs.
  • To provide food and education to orphaned and underprivileged children.
  • To promote awareness about Netaji’s life through media and literature.
  • To offer self-defense and life skill training for women and children.
  • To conduct competitions and seminars promoting patriotism.
  • To conduct cultural programs in government schools and institutions.
  • To raise awareness about Netaji through research, books, and documentaries.
  • To offer scholarships and books to underprivileged students.
  • To raise public participation in national development and legal awareness.
  • To form BNF volunteer teams in every district and village.
  • To establish Netaji Leadership Training Camps.
  • To uphold nationalism and national unity in society.
  • To promote youth leadership for nation-building.
  • To uplift the underprivileged through education and welfare.
  • To provide social protection training for women and children.
  • To organize national/international programs & festivals on Netaji.
  • To create a new India driven by Netaji’s values and patriotism.

What We Focus On

  • Encouraging young people to join the armed forces
  • Organizing training workshops on patriotism and community service
  • Reaching and assisting rural communities unaware of government welfare schemes
  • Conducting sports competitions in the name of Netaji
  • Providing educational assistance – including books and scholarships
  • Implementing village development projects like solar lamps, sanitation, and healthcare
  • Presenting the Netaji Award to recognize outstanding social service
  • Running a Netaji Library / Historical Document Collection Project
  • Offering free medical consultations and 25% assistance for police and armed forces personnel
  • Providing free Silambam martial arts classes in all districts
  • Operating a free ambulance service for poor and underprivileged patients
  • Offering free funeral carriage service for the needy
